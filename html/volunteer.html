How to Volunteer

Do you ever wonder what happens to dining hall food leftovers? Campus Kitchens at Georgia Tech makes use of this food by preparing meals to fill our campus food pantry, Klemis Kitchen, and to feed Atlanta's homeless. We rely on volunteers to keep Klemis fully stocked for students in need!

You can sign up for shifts on any Tuesday from 7:45 - 8:45 pm or any Friday from 3:30 to 4:30 pm at Britain Dining Hall.
We ask that volunteers sign up for shifts through our Orgsync page.
