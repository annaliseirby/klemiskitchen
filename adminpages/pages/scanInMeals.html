Instructions


1. Click inside the Scan Here box.
2. Use barcode scanner to scan the item you would like to enter in the database. When you do, the name of the item should appear in the Current Items box.
3. If you would like to remove an item, click on the item inside the Current Item box and click the Remove button.
4. When you are done adding and removing items, click the Enter Meals into Database button below to submit the items to the database.
